<p><p>The Skim Milk Powder (SMP) market is categorized into three types based on processing temperatures: Low Heat SMP, Medium Heat SMP, and High Heat SMP. Low Heat SMP is produced at low temperatures and retains a high level of functionality, making it suitable for reconstitution in beverages. Medium Heat SMP is processed at moderate temperatures and is often used in dairy products and bakery applications. High Heat SMP undergoes extensive processing, resulting in enhanced solubility and stability, ideal for confectionery and infant formulas.</p></p>
