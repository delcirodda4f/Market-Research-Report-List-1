<p><p>Parenteral nutrition (PN) is a medical method of delivering essential nutrients directly into the bloodstream, bypassing the digestive tract. This approach is crucial for patients who cannot obtain adequate nutrition through oral intake due to various conditions such as gastrointestinal disorders, postoperative recovery, or severe malnutrition. PN typically includes a mix of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals, tailored to meet individual patient needs.</p><p>The Parenteral Nutrition Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by an increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, rising hospitalization rates, and the growing elderly population, which often requires specialized nutritional support. Additionally, advancements in technology related to the formulation and administration of parenteral nutrients are enhancing safety and efficacy, attracting more healthcare facilities to implement these solutions. </p><p>Recent trends in the market include a shift towards personalized nutrition, where treatments are customized based on genetic and metabolic profiles. Furthermore, there is an increasing focus on home parenteral nutrition, allowing patients greater flexibility and improved quality of life. The rising awareness and education about nutrition in healthcare settings are also pivotal in fostering market expansion.</p></p>
