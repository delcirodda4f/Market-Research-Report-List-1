<p><p>The lubricant for metal forming market comprises various types tailored for specific processes, including tube drawing lubricants and hot forming lubricants. Tube drawing lubricants are designed to facilitate the drawing of metal tubes, reducing friction and enhancing surface finish, thereby improving material flow and extending tool life. Hot forming lubricants, on the other hand, are utilized in high-temperature processes, providing excellent lubrication under extreme conditions, minimizing wear, and preventing oxidation, which is crucial for achieving desired shapes and maintaining product integrity.</p></p>
