<p><p>The wearable devices UV sensors market is categorized into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors detect long-wave ultraviolet rays, primarily linked to skin aging and prolonged exposure effects. UVB sensors measure medium-wave ultraviolet rays, responsible for sunburn and more severe skin damage. UVC sensors focus on short-wave ultraviolet rays, which are mainly harmful in artificial settings, like disinfecting surfaces. Each type serves distinct purposes in health monitoring and environmental safety, aiding users in sun exposure management.</p></p>
