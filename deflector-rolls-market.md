<p><p>Deflector rolls in the market are categorized into hot rolled and cold rolled types based on their manufacturing processes. Hot rolled deflector rolls are produced at high temperatures, resulting in improved ductility and a more uniform thickness, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, cold rolled deflector rolls undergo processing at room temperature, providing enhanced precision, surface finish, and strength, ideal for applications requiring tighter tolerances. Each type serves distinct industrial needs and operational efficiencies.</p></p>
