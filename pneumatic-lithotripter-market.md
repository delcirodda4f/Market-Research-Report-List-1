<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Lithotripter without Ultrasonic</li><li>Ultrasonic Pneumatic Lithotripter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pneumatic Lithotripter market is categorized primarily into two types: Pneumatic Lithotripters without Ultrasonic and Ultrasonic Pneumatic Lithotripters. Pneumatic Lithotripters without Ultrasonic utilize compressed air to fragment kidney stones, focusing solely on mechanical energy. In contrast, Ultrasonic Pneumatic Lithotripters combine pneumatic technology with ultrasonic waves, enhancing stone fragmentation efficiency through vibrational energy. Both types offer unique advantages in urological procedures, impacting their adoption based on factors like effectiveness, patient safety, and operational costs in medical facilities.</p></p>
