<p><p>The mica cable tape market comprises primarily two types: mica glass fiber tape and mica polyester tape. Mica glass fiber tape combines the heat-resistant properties of mica with the durability of glass fiber, making it suitable for applications requiring high thermal stability and mechanical strength. In contrast, mica polyester tape offers flexibility and insulation, ideal for environments where resistance to chemicals and moisture is crucial. Both types are essential in electrical and electronic industries for safe and efficient operation of cables.</p></p>
