<p><ul><li>Positive Pressure Isolation Room</li><li>Negative Pressure Isolation Room</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Modular Isolation Room market encompasses two primary types: Positive Pressure Isolation Rooms and Negative Pressure Isolation Rooms. Positive Pressure Isolation Rooms are designed to protect patients with weakened immune systems by preventing outside contaminants from entering the room. In contrast, Negative Pressure Isolation Rooms are utilized to contain and prevent the spread of airborne pathogens, ensuring that any infectious agents remain within the room. Both types play a crucial role in infection control within healthcare settings, catering to specific patient care needs.</p></p>
