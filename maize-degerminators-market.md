<p><strong>The Maize Degerminators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Maize degerminators can be classified into horizontal and vertical types based on their design and functionality. Horizontal degerminators utilize a rotating mechanism to effectively separate the germ and endosperm from maize kernels, optimizing processing efficiency. In contrast, vertical degerminators employ a gravity-based approach, allowing kernels to fall through a series of stages where separation occurs. Both types cater to different processing needs and capacities, impacting the overall efficiency and output quality in maize milling operations.</p></p>
