<p><strong>The Hydroelectric Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbine</li><li>Reaction Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydroelectric generator market features two primary turbine types: impulse and reaction turbines. Impulse turbines operate by directing water jets onto blades, converting kinetic energy into mechanical energy, making them suitable for high-head applications. Reaction turbines, on the other hand, utilize the pressure difference between water entering and exiting the turbine, functioning effectively in low to medium-head scenarios. Both types play crucial roles in hydroelectric power generation, catering to diverse hydrological conditions and energy demand across various regions.</p></p>
