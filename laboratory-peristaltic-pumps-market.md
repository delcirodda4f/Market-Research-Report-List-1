<p><p>Laboratory peristaltic pumps are categorized into two main types: fixed-speed pumps and variable-speed pumps. Fixed-speed pumps deliver a constant flow rate, making them ideal for applications requiring consistent fluid transfer. In contrast, variable-speed pumps offer adjustable flow rates, providing greater flexibility for different experimental setups and varying liquid viscosities. This adaptability is crucial in laboratories where precise fluid management is essential for accurate results, facilitating diverse applications from chemical dosing to cell culture maintenance.</p></p>
