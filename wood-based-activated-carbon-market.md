<p><p>The wood-based activated carbon market is categorized into three main types: Powdered Activated Carbon (PAC), Granular Activated Carbon (GAC), and Others. PAC is characterized by fine particles, ideal for applications requiring rapid adsorption, such as water treatment and air purification. GAC features larger granules, offering a longer-lasting solution for filtering and adsorption in various industries. The 'Others' category includes specialized forms like extruded or pelletized carbon, designed for niche applications such as gas masks and industrial processes.</p></p>
