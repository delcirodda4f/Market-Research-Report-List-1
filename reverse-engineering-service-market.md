<p><p>The reverse engineering service market encompasses three primary types: physical reversal, software reverse engineering, and image reverse engineering. Physical reversal involves analyzing physical products to recreate their design or functionality, often for improvement or replication. Software reverse engineering focuses on deconstructing software applications to understand code structure, optimize performance, or patch vulnerabilities. Image reverse engineering deals with analyzing and reconstructing visual data, such as photographs or digital images, to extract insights or create new designs. Each type serves distinct purposes across various industries.</p></p>
