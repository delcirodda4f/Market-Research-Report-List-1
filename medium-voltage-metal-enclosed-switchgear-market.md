<p><p>The Medium Voltage Metal-Enclosed Switchgear market consists of three primary types: </p><p>1. **Air Insulated Metal-Enclosed Switchgear (AIS)**, which utilizes air as the insulating medium, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for outdoor installations.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Gas Insulated Metal-Enclosed Switchgear (GIS)**, which employs sulfur hexafluoride gas for insulation, providing compact and reliable solutions, ideal for space-constrained environments.</p><p>3. **Solid Insulated Metal-Enclosed Switchgear (SIS)**, designed with solid insulation materials, offering enhanced safety and environmental benefits, suitable for various applications requiring high reliability and reduced maintenance.</p></p>
