<p><p>The transplantation therapeutics drugs market comprises several key categories. Calcineurin inhibitors, such as cyclosporine and tacrolimus, prevent organ rejection by inhibiting T-cell activation. Anti-proliferative agents, like azathioprine and mycophenolate mofetil, suppress cell growth to reduce immune responses. Corticosteroids, including prednisone, provide rapid immune modulation to control inflammation and prevent rejection. Additionally, "Others" encompass diverse agents such as mTOR inhibitors and monoclonal antibodies, contributing to a comprehensive approach in enhancing transplant success and patient outcomes.</p></p>
