<p><strong>The Car Wheel Rims Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubeless Wheel Rims</li><li>Tube Wheel Rims</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car wheel rims market is primarily divided into two types: tubeless wheel rims and tube wheel rims. Tubeless wheel rims are designed to hold air without an inner tube, offering benefits like reduced weight, lower rolling resistance, and better fuel efficiency. They are commonly used in modern vehicles for their safety and convenience. In contrast, tube wheel rims require an inner tube to contain air, traditionally used in older vehicle models. These rims can be easier to repair but are generally less favored in contemporary automotive design.</p></p>
