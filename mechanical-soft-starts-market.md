<p><p>Mechanical soft starters are essential devices that gradually introduce power to motors, reducing inrush current and mechanical stress. The market features various types, notably fluid couplings and Flexidyne couplings. Fluid couplings utilize hydraulic fluid to transmit torque smoothly, allowing for soft starts and better load handling. Flexidyne couplings, on the other hand, employ a blending of mechanical and hydraulic principles, ensuring precise speed control and shock absorption. Together, they enhance operational efficiency and prolong equipment lifespan in various industrial applications.</p></p>
