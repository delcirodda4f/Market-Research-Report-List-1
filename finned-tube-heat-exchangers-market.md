<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Type</li><li>Transverse Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Finned tube heat exchangers are categorized into two main types: longitudinal and transverse. Longitudinal type exchangers have fins aligned with the flow direction, improving heat transfer efficiency while maintaining compact design, making them ideal for applications with limited space. In contrast, transverse type exchangers feature fins arranged perpendicular to the flow, enhancing performance in larger systems where air or fluid moves in a crosswise manner. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs, optimizing thermal management in various processes.</p></p>
