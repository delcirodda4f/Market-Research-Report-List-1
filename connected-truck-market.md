<p><ul><li>Dedicated Short Range</li><li>Long Range/Cellular Network</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Connected Truck Market encompasses two main types: Dedicated Short Range and Long Range/Cellular Network. Dedicated Short Range communication uses specialized, low-latency connections for applications like vehicle-to-vehicle communication, enhancing safety and efficiency in close proximity. In contrast, Long Range/Cellular Network connectivity leverages existing cellular infrastructure to provide broader coverage, enabling real-time data exchange for fleet management, navigation, and diagnostics over long distances. Together, these technologies enhance operational efficiency, safety, and connectivity in the trucking industry.</p></p>
