<p><p>The ophthalmic surgical systems market is primarily divided into two key types: laser ophthalmic surgical systems and phacoemulsification ophthalmic surgical systems. Laser ophthalmic surgical systems utilize advanced laser technology for procedures such as LASIK and cataract surgery, offering precision and reduced recovery times. In contrast, phacoemulsification ophthalmic surgical systems focus on breaking up and removing cataracts using ultrasonic vibrations, followed by lens implantation. Both types enhance surgical outcomes and improve patient experiences in treating various eye conditions.</p></p>
