<p><p>An Ultrasonic Flowmeter is a precision instrument used to measure the flow rate of liquids and gases by utilizing ultrasonic signals. It operates on the principle of sound wave propagation through a medium, measuring the time it takes for an ultrasonic pulse to travel upstream and downstream.
