<p><strong>The TBM Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tail Sealants</li><li>Main Drive Sealants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TBM sealants market comprises two primary types: tail sealants and main drive sealants. Tail sealants are used to seal the gap between the tunnel boring machine (TBM) and the tunnel wall, preventing water and soil ingress. Main drive sealants, on the other hand, are applied in the TBM's main drive section to ensure proper sealing and lubrication of moving parts, enhancing operational efficiency and extending machine longevity. Both types are essential for safe and effective tunneling operations.</p></p>
