<p><p>The fat replacers market is categorized into three main types: carbohydrate-based, protein-based, and lipid-based fat replacers, along with other formulations. Protein-based replacers often incorporate whey or soy proteins to enhance texture and nutritional value. Lipid-based fat replacers mimic fats using limited-fat sources or non-digestible fats, providing flavor and creaminess. Other types may include a combination of these or novel ingredients catering to specific consumer preferences and dietary needs.</p></p>
