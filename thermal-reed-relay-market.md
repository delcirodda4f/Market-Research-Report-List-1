<p><p>A Thermal Reed Relay is an electrical switch that uses the principle of thermal expansion to operate. It comprises a pair of ferromagnetic reeds encased in a sealed glass tube. When the temperature exceeds a certain threshold, the reeds come into contact, completing the circuit. <p><p>The thermal reed relay market features three main types: Normally Open (H), Normally Closed (D), and Transformation (Z). Normally Open relays activate under specific conditions, allowing current flow when energized. Normally Closed relays maintain current flow until triggered, thus providing a default state. Transformation relays adapt circuit configurations, enabling versatile applications. Each type serves distinct needs in automation and control systems, catering to diverse industries and facilitating efficient electrical management through reliable switching mechanisms.</p></p>
