<p><strong>The RF Test Probe Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Type</li><li>Active Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF test probe market is divided into two main types: passive and active probes. Passive probes are simple devices that rely on resistance and capacitance to measure signals without adding significant loading effects, making them ideal for high-frequency measurements. In contrast, active probes incorporate amplifiers, providing higher sensitivity and the ability to measure smaller signals with minimal distortion, but they may introduce some loading. Both types are crucial for accurate RF signal testing across various applications, including telecommunications and electronics.</p></p>
