<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Train Operation</li><li>Driverless Train Operation</li><li>Unattended Train Operation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Urban Rail Signalling Systems market encompasses various operational types, including Semi-Automatic Train Operation (STO), Driverless Train Operation (DTO), and Unattended Train Operation (UTO). STO involves trains that require some human intervention for operations, enhancing efficiency and safety. DTO eliminates the need for onboard drivers, relying on advanced automation technologies for optimal performance. UTO extends this further by operating trains without any personnel present, relying entirely on automated systems for scheduling, monitoring, and control, thus maximizing operational efficiency and safety.</p></p>
