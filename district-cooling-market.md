<p><ul><li>Free Cooling</li><li>Absorption Cooling</li><li>Electric Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The District Cooling market encompasses three main types: Free Cooling, Absorption Cooling, and Electric Chillers. Free Cooling utilizes ambient air or water to lower temperatures efficiently, reducing energy consumption during cooler months. Absorption Cooling leverages heat sources, such as natural gas or waste heat, to drive the cooling process, making it environmentally friendly. Electric Chillers, on the other hand, use electricity to provide cooling through vapor compression, offering flexibility and scalability in urban developments. These systems collectively enhance energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
